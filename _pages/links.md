This is a list of useful resources and links.

## Consensus papers

Throughout 2019 and 2020, NMR in Biomedicine has published several expert consensus papers from various areas of MRS.

[Advanced single voxel 1H magnetic resonance spectroscopy techniques in humans: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4236)

[Terminology and concepts for the characterization of in vivo MR spectroscopy methods and MR spectra: Background and experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4347)

[Preprocessing, analysis and quantification in single-voxel magnetic resonance spectroscopy: experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4257)

[B0 shimming for in vivo magnetic resonance spectroscopy: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4350)

[Spectral editing in 1H magnetic resonance spectroscopy: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4411)

[Advanced magnetic resonance spectroscopic neuroimaging: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4309)

[Motion correction methods for MRS: experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4364)

[31P magnetic resonance spectroscopy in skeletal muscle: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4246)

[Proton magnetic resonance spectroscopy in skeletal muscle: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4266)

[Magnetic resonance spectroscopy in the rodent brain: Experts' consensus recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4325)

[Proton functional magnetic resonance spectroscopy in rodents](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/nbm.4254)

Additionally, there is an excellent consensus on clinical MRS methods in Magnetic Resonance in Medicine:

[Methodological consensus on clinical proton MRS of the brain: Review and recommendations](https://onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/full/10.1002/mrm.27742)
